title: "ADR 0001: License Posture"
description: Apache-2.0 for SDKs/adapters/templates and AGPL-3.0 for server-side components.
---

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

UBAG uses Apache-2.0 for SDKs, adapters, and templates, and AGPL-3.0 for server-side platform components.

## Consequences

SDKs remain adoption-friendly for proprietary applications. Server-side platform improvements remain protected against closed hosted forks.
